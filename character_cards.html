Alan Turing

Father of modern computing, known for his work on theoretical computer science and artificial intelligence.

Grace Hopper

Pioneer in computer programming, known for her work on developing the first compiler.

Claude Shannon

Founder of information theory, which laid the foundation for digital communication.

Ada Lovelace

First computer programmer, known for her work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical general-purpose computer.

John von Neumann

Mathematician who made major contributions to computer architecture, including the concept of the stored-program computer.
