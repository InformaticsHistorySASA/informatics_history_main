Alan Turing

튜링은 아주 유명한 정보과학의 아빠이자 유명한 게이고 독사과를 먹어 자살한것으로 유명함

Grace Hopper

Pioneer in computer programming, known for her work on developing the first compiler.

Claude Shannon

Founder of information theory, which laid the foundation for digital communication.

Ada Lovelace

First computer programmer, known for her work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical general-purpose computer.

John von Neumann

Mathematician who made major contributions to computer architecture, including the concept of the stored-program computer.

Donald Knuth

Author of "The Art of Computer Programming" and a pioneer in algorithm analysis.

Barbara Liskov

Noted for her work in programming languages and software engineering, and the Liskov substitution principle.

Tim Berners-Lee

Inventor of the World Wide Web, which revolutionized how we access and share information globally.
